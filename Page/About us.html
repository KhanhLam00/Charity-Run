Our Story

Charity Run 2024 was founded with a mission to support the community through charitable activities, particularly through running events. We aim to provide educational opportunities and a brighter future for children in remote areas.

Over the years, we have organized numerous successful fundraising events, spreading the message of compassion and unity within the community.

Vision and Mission

Our mission is to build a healthy and compassionate community. Through Charity Run, we seek donations to help those in need, focusing on improving educational conditions for children in highland areas.

Our vision for Charity Run 2024 is to continue organizing annual running events, attracting contributions to build libraries and improve educational environments for children in remote areas across the country.
