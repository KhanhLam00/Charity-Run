How to Participate in the Charity Run

Welcome to our Charity Run for Building Libraries for Children in Remote Areas at Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center (SECC), Hochiminh City, on 11th October 2024. Whether you want to run, volunteer, or sponsor the event, we have comprehensive guides and information below.

1. As a Runner

How to Register:

Online Registration:

  • Visit our official website.
  • Complete the registration form with your personal information (Name, Email, Contact Number).
  • Choose your running distance: 5km, 10km, or 21km (Half Marathon).
  • Complete payment through our online payment portal (Credit card, e-wallets).
  • After registration, you’ll receive a confirmation email with your bib number and event details.

On-site Registration: You can also register directly at SECC's Reception Area on the event day from 06:00 - 06:30 AM.

Participation Fees:

  • 5km: 150,000 VNĐ
  • 10km: 250,000 VNĐ
  • 21km (Half Marathon): 400,000 VNĐ
  • Early Bird Discount: 10% off for registrations before 1st October 2024.

Event Schedule for Runners:

  • 06:00 - 06:30: Registration and Bib Collection (SECC Reception Area).
  • 06:30 - 07:00: Warm-up and Preparation (Warm-up Area).
  • 07:00 - 07:15: Opening Ceremony (Main Stage).
  • 07:15 - 08:00: Race Start (5km, 10km, 21km distances) on Designated Running Tracks.
  • 09:00 - 09:30: Award Ceremony for Top Runners (Main Stage).

2. As a Volunteer

How to Register:

  • Sign up online through our website or at SECC’s Reception Area on event day.

Roles include:

  • Water Station Assistants: Help distribute water to runners at designated stations.
  • Route Guides: Assist runners in staying on the correct route and ensure safety.
  • Medical Support Team: (Requires basic first-aid knowledge) Aid runners in case of emergencies.

Volunteer Benefits: Volunteers will receive a free event shirt, certificate of participation, and snacks.

3. As a Sponsor

Sponsorship Packages:

We offer various sponsorship levels for companies and individuals interested in supporting the event:

Package Benefits Price
Bronze Sponsor Logo displayed on event banners and website 5 million VNĐ
Silver Sponsor Logo on banners, website, and event t-shirts 10 million VNĐ
Gold Sponsor Logo on all event materials, including banners, website, t-shirts, and recognition at the opening ceremony 20 million VNĐ
Platinum Sponsor All Gold Sponsor benefits, plus an opportunity to speak at the opening ceremony and have a promotional booth at the event 50 million VNĐ or more

Space Rental Opportunities:

Businesses can rent exhibition booths at the event for 2 million VNĐ per 3x3m booth. Booths will be located at SECC’s Side Areas, which will host health consultations and reading activities from 08:00 - 09:00 AM.

Event Safety Guidelines:

  • Health Requirements: All participants must ensure they are in good health. Medical support will be available throughout the event.
  • Event Security: Follow all safety instructions from staff and security personnel to ensure a smooth and safe experience for everyone.
  • Refund Policy: In case of unforeseen circumstances, such as extreme weather, participants can request a 50% refund or transfer their registration to the next event.

For more details, please contact our team or visit the FAQs section on our website. We look forward to seeing you at SECC for a successful charity run and meaningful community contribution!
