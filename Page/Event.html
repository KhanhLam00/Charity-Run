Event Objectives

Main Objective:

Fundraising for a library: We call on community support to build a small library at a remote elementary school, where resources for learning are scarce. This event aims to provide better learning conditions for children, helping them access knowledge and develop themselves.

Secondary Objectives:

  • Raise awareness: We want everyone to understand the importance of reading and education for children, helping them expand their knowledge and grow.
  • Encourage physical activity: This event is not just a run; it’s an opportunity for all ages to participate in sports activities, fostering a more connected community.

Activities in the Event

Fundraising Run

Running distances: 5km, 10km, and 21km, suitable for beginners and professional athletes alike. Choose the distance that suits your ability and participate!

Prizes: Awards will be given to top achievers in each distance category, including medals, attractive gifts, and participation certificates. Compete to win deserving prizes!

Location: 799 Nguyen Van Linh, Tan Phu Ward, Dist. 7, Hochiminh City

Side Activities

  • Health consultation area: Experts will be present to provide advice on nutrition, health,and effective training methods.
  • A dedicated space for children with books and engaging storytelling activities, helping them explore the world of literature.
