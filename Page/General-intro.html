Run for the Future

October 11, 2024 - Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center (SECC)

Event Information

Venue: SECC - 799 Nguyễn Văn Linh, Tân Phú Ward, District 7, Ho Chi Minh City

Date: October 11, 2024

Venue Access and Directions

The event will be held at the Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center (SECC), located in District 7, Ho Chi Minh City. It is approximately 30 minutes from the city center and can be easily reached via Nguyễn Văn Linh Boulevard.

GPS Address: 799 Nguyễn Văn Linh, Tân Phú Ward, District 7, Ho Chi Minh City

View Map & Directions

Parking Facilities

  • On-site Parking: SECC offers a large parking area with capacity for up to 1,000 vehicles, including cars and motorbikes.
  • Fees: Free for event participants. Non-participants will be charged 20,000 VND for motorbikes and 50,000 VND for cars.
  • Overflow Parking: Additional parking is available just 5 minutes walking distance from SECC.

Public Transportation Options

  • Bus: Bus lines 20 and 72C stop near SECC, only a 10-minute walk from the event venue.
  • Taxi and Ride-hailing: Services such as Grab and BeCar can take you directly to the SECC entrance.
  • Shuttle Service: Free shuttle buses are available from the City Opera House (District 1) to SECC, departing every 30 minutes from 5:30 AM to 9:00 AM on the event day.

Special Access

SECC has dedicated access points and parking for disabled participants. Volunteers will be available to assist if needed.

Special Entitlements for Participants

Participant Kits

All registered participants will receive a free event kit, including a branded T-shirt, water bottle, race bib, and finisher's medal.

Kit collection time: 5:30 AM to 6:30 AM on October 11, 2024, at the registration desk near the SECC entrance. Participants are advised to arrive early to complete registration and collect their kits before the event starts. Please bring your confirmation email and identification to collect your kit.

Food & Refreshments

Complimentary snacks and drinks will be available throughout the event, including water, energy drinks, and fruits at rest stations along the route. After finishing the race, each participant will receive a free meal box containing a sandwich, fruit, and a beverage.

VIP Access

VIP participants will have access to a private lounge equipped with seating, air conditioning, and refreshments. VIP registration includes priority parking and special gifts from sponsors.

Discounts

Participants will enjoy a 10% discount on event merchandise, including hats and backpacks, at exhibitor booths throughout the event.

Event Exhibitors and Partners

Exhibitors

  • Nike: Showcasing the latest running apparel with exclusive discounts for participants.
  • Fit4Life: Offering free fitness consultations and mini workout sessions.
  • Herbalife Nutrition: Providing free samples of nutrition supplements and drinks.

Partners

We are proud to partner with various organizations supporting health, fitness, and charity:

  • Vietnam Red Cross: Organizing a blood donation program at the event.
  • Vietnam Health Fund: Raising awareness about children's health in disadvantaged areas.

Sponsors

Main Sponsors

  • Vinamilk: As the main sponsor, Vinamilk will provide free milk and drinks for all participants, along with a booth showcasing healthy nutrition products.
  • Vietcombank: Vietcombank, our financial sponsor, will offer exciting gifts for participants who open new accounts during the event.

Sponsor Activities

  • Vinamilk Health Zone: Join Vinamilk's booth for free health check-ups and nutrition advice.
  • Vietcombank Lucky Draw: Register at Vietcombank's booth for a chance to win cash prizes or shopping vouchers.
